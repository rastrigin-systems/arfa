## üéØ The Vision

### What We're Building

**Ubik Enterprise is the centralized management platform for AI coding agents in the workplace.**

Think **Okta for AI agents** - companies use us to manage, control, and secure access to Claude Code, Cursor, Windsurf, and other AI coding assistants across their entire organization.

### The Core Insight

**The Problem:**
- Companies want to give developers AI coding assistants
- But they can't just hand out API keys to everyone
- They need control, visibility, compliance, and cost management
- Current solution: Don't adopt AI, or let it be the Wild West

**Our Solution:**
- IT configures AI agents once in our platform
- Employees type one command: `ubik`
- They get the full AI coding experience
- IT gets complete control, visibility, and compliance

**The Magic:**
> Employees don't know we exist. IT loves us.

---

**Tier 3: Enterprise (Custom)**
- Custom pricing (typically $50-100/developer/month)
- 500+ developers
- Everything in Professional, plus:
  - Custom integrations
  - Dedicated support
  - On-premise deployment option
  - SLA guarantees
  - Compliance reports
  - Custom contracts

**Target:** Large enterprises, financial services, healthcare

---

### Revenue Model Options

**Option A: Pure Per-Seat (Simple)**
- Fixed price per developer per month
- We absorb AI API costs
- Predictable for customers
- Easier to sell

**Pros:** Simple, predictable, enterprise-friendly
**Cons:** We take on cost risk

---

**Option B: Per-Seat + Usage (Transparent)**
- Base price: $25/developer/month (platform fee)
- Plus: AI API costs pass-through + 20% margin
- Customers see exact AI costs
- We make money on volume

**Pros:** Scales with usage, low risk for us
**Cons:** Unpredictable bills for customers

---

**Option C: Hybrid (Best of Both)**
- Tier 1-2: Fixed per-seat pricing
- Tier 3: Base fee + usage-based
- Gives predictability to small customers
- Scales with large customers

**Recommendation:** Start with Option A (simple), move to Option C at scale.
